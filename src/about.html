Maths is easy, design is hard.

I like both. Making a good design costs a lot of efforts and time, because my job is creating normalcy out of chaos. I have the responsibility to communicate your ideas through organising and manipulating words and pictures. But as a developer, I have also the duty to build a solid backbone for your project, something that you won't ever be aware until something goes wrong.

This is the path I have chosen to take. The first time I heard the words 'Designing for emotions' I got trapped in this world of beauty, art and coffes. My first lines of code sounded like a promise of everlasting artworks, fights against people's problems and headaches.

What am I currently looking for eagerly?

I would like to join an inspiring project, a team of professionals in order to improve my skills and to know like-minded colleagues.

How I work

I like building from scratch, following my creativity and experience. I prefer choosing on my own which tools are needed and provide the best solution that suits you.

I am in fact an experimenter, always working with the edge of knowledge in order to keep updated with the last trends. I love testing new tools and trying to get the best from each.
